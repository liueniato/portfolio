Welcome to Sip Green

Your Eco-Friendly Source for Bamboo Straws

About Us

Sip Green is a company dedicated to reducing plastic waste by providing eco-friendly bamboo straws for coffee shops. We source our bamboo from Asia using sustainable transportation methods like trains, ensuring a minimal carbon footprint.


Established in 2023, Sip Green Oy was founded by a team of six business students from Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences: Aletta, Alli, Chike, Khai, Lavi, and Joni. Together, we bring a collective vision of environmental stewardship and social responsibility to the forefront of our enterprise.


As a start-up company, we are firmly focused on the limitless potential of the future. We are in cooperation with One Tree Planted, a non-profit organization focused on global reforestation. By donating some when buying our product, you can trust that part of the amount you just paid is directed to a good cause to fight the climate change.

Our Products

At Sip Green, we take immense pride in crafting our bamboo straws from 100% natural bamboo trees. We only use branches that have fallen to the ground without damaging the tree itself. These straws are the epitome of excellence, designed with the discerning environmentally conscious coffee shop in mind. Renowned for their biodegradability and durability, our bamboo straws are the perfect complement to elevate your beverage experience.

Our Mission

  1. Reducing Plastic Waste: We understand the detrimental effects of plastic waste on our planet. By offering an alternative to plastic straws, we aim to contribute to the reduction of plastic pollution in oceans and landfills.
  2. Promoting Sustainability: We take sustainability seriously. Our bamboo straws are sourced from Asia using sustainable transportation methods, such as trains, to minimize our carbon footprint. We are continually exploring ways to improve the sustainability of our supply chain. This includes possible storage transition into EU/EEA area.
  3. Supporting Local Communities: Sip Green is not just about sustainability; it's also about people. We work closely with local communities in Asia to source our bamboo, providing fair livelihoods for those involved in the production process.
  4. Innovating for a Better Future: We are committed to innovation and constantly researching new ways to enhance our products and reduce our environmental impact. Our goal is to be at the forefront of eco-friendly solutions for coffee shops worldwide.
